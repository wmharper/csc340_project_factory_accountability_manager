FAM: Supervisor Dashboard

Log Out Send Message View Database

In this container,

the supervisor is capable of seeing the status of all the lines he or she is overseeing.

This is where they can click a button that will bring the production to a halt, and tell employees to restart it.

For our purposes, all this does is reseed the generator for the line, in the hopes that it will run better after a restart.
