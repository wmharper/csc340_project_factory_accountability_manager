In this container,

The user will interact with our "Inspection" program.

The inspection program is designed to simulate the inspection of products as they are produced by an assembly line.

At the startup of an assembly line, a seed is created that will decide the range of qualities of product that will be produced.

With a low seed, the line is likely to produce many defective products. Employees can send a message to a supervisor to alert them of an assembly line's malfunction.

The employee is shown a product with a randomly assigned quality value, normally distributed around the seed generated at the production line startup.

The employee is expected to determine whether the product meets the necessary product quality thresholds, and if it is, then it is approved to down the line (the employee clicks a button). If it is not, the employee throws the product into a shredder to reuse the material.
